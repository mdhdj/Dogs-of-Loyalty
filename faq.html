Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our dog training products and services

General Questions

What is Dogs of Loyalty?

Dogs of Loyalty is a resource dedicated to sharing heartwarming true stories of canine loyalty and providing effective, science-based dog training solutions. We offer digital training programs designed to help dog owners build stronger relationships with their pets through positive reinforcement methods.

Are your training methods humane and effective?

Yes, absolutely. All of our training programs are based on positive reinforcement techniques that are scientifically proven to be both humane and effective. We do not endorse or teach any methods that involve punishment, fear, or intimidation. Our approach focuses on building trust and communication between you and your dog.

Do I need any special equipment for your training programs?

Most of our training programs require minimal equipment - typically just a standard leash, collar or harness, and some high-value treats. Specific equipment needs are outlined in each product description. None of our programs require specialized or expensive tools.

Product Questions

What's included in your training programs?

Each of our training programs includes comprehensive materials designed to give you everything you need:

  • ✓ Detailed step-by-step guides (digital manuals)
  • ✓ Video demonstrations of each technique
  • ✓ Printable training schedules and checklists
  • ✓ Access to exclusive support resources
  • ✓ Bonus materials specific to each program

How soon will I see results with your training programs?

Results vary depending on your dog's age, breed, previous training, and consistency of implementation. Many customers report noticeable improvements within the first week of consistent training. For more complex behaviors, it may take several weeks. The key is consistency and patience - our programs provide clear timelines for each training objective.

Can your programs work for older dogs?

Yes! While puppies often learn more quickly, dogs of any age can learn new behaviors. Our programs include specific guidance for working with adult and senior dogs, taking into account potential physical limitations and established habits. "You can't teach an old dog new tricks" is simply not true with the right approach.

What's the difference between your various training programs?

Each of our programs focuses on specific training goals:

  • ✓ Secrets to Dog Training: Comprehensive foundation for basic obedience and communication
  • ✓ Brain Training for Dogs: Focuses on mental stimulation, problem-solving, and advanced cognitive exercises
  • ✓ Housetraining Any Dog: Specialized system for potty training puppies and resolving accidents in adult dogs

Many customers purchase multiple programs to address different aspects of their dog's training needs.

Purchasing & Support

How do I access my purchased products?

After purchase, you'll receive an email with download links and access instructions. You can also log into your account on our website to access your products anytime. Digital products are available immediately after purchase.

What is your refund policy?

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee. If you're not satisfied with your purchase for any reason, contact us within 30 days for a full refund. No questions asked. We want you to feel confident in your investment in your dog's training.

Do you offer personalized support?

Yes, all our training programs include access to email support from our certified dog trainers. For more personalized guidance, we offer Custom Training Consultations (available as a separate service through our contact page).

Can I share my purchased materials with others?

Our digital products are licensed for individual use only. Sharing download links or materials with others violates our terms of service. If friends or family members are interested in our programs, we encourage them to purchase their own copies, which helps us continue creating high-quality content.

Do you have a physical book or DVD option?

Currently, all our training materials are available digitally for immediate access. Digital format allows us to keep our prices affordable while providing the most up-to-date information. However, you can print the manuals if you prefer a physical copy.
